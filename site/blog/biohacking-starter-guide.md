# Biohacking Starter Guide — 10 quick wins

Short practical wins you can do this week to feel better and focus more.

## 1. Fix your sleep window
- Keep consistent wake time for 7 days.

## 2. Morning light
- 10 minutes near a window or a daylight lamp.

## 3. Blue-light blockers in the evening
- Wear blue-light glasses after sunset.

## 4. Short evening routine
- 20 minutes of low-stim activities (read, stretch).

## 5. Cold exposure (start small)
- 30–60s cold shower at the end of your shower.

## 6. Caffeine timing
- Avoid caffeine after 14:00 local time.

## 7. Focus blocks
- 90-minute blocks with 10–15 min breaks.

## 8. Movement microbreaks
- 5 minutes every hour to mobilise.

## 9. Supplements (conservative)
- Magnesium glycinate at night; consult GP.

## 10. Minimal tracking
- Track one metric for 14 days (sleep or focus).
